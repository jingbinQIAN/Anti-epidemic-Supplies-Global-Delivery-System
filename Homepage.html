Foreword

With the process of globalization, more and more Chinese youth choose to study abroad. However, the outbreak of COVID-19 made studying abroad hard and dangerous.
In order to protect our Chinese students, we build this website to deliver the anti-epidemic supplies
Through our system, international Chinese students can make orders for anti-epidemic materials and check the delivery information. Our Delivery Center in different countries can establish cooperation with suppliers, oversee the logistics process and guarantee the needs for anti-epidemic material of each Chinese student in its jurisdiction. Deliverymen can view the transportation information as well as the contact information of students.
